About Me Story

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Here's a little bit about me.

My interest in this industry began with the rudimentary Photoshop skills my mother taught me when I was young. From that day on, computers became a part of my life, and my interests expanded beyond design into various creative fields like programming.
Two years ago, I moved to Canada alone and studied design and development professionally at Fanshawe College, further expanding my creative horizons. I aspire to be a bridge, delivering your vision to users through the medium of design.
I promise to dedicate my full effort to making this a reality.
