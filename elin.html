Elin Case Study

Elin

Developed a full brand identity for Elin, an imaginary skincare brand inspired by camellia flowers. The project included branding, graphic design, and video commercials showcasing Elin's gentle, natural, and modern approach.

Project Period

4 Weeks

Role

Graphic Designer / Motion Designer

Tools

Photoshop / Illustorator / After Effects

Collaborate With

Linh Nguyen

Challenges

The main challenge was differentiating the brand from the countless other skincare companies on the market and building a brand identity that is unique while still remaining clean and simple.
To achieve this, I established a strong brand story before moving into the design phase and developed a visual concept that truly resonates with the target audience.

In the promotional video, I focused on maintaining a cohesive tone while conveying the background of the brand in a concise manner, elevating it into an advertising piece that represents Elin as a whole.
