Kids TV Channel Bumper

Kids TV Channel Bumper

Created a TV bumper for a kids channel with full 3D animation in Cinema 4D, delivering a playful and engaging visual style that reflects the channel's fun and energetic identity.

Project Period

2 Weeks

Role

Motion Designer

Tools

Cinema 4D / After Effects

*This work is a student project created for educational purposes only. All trademarks, logos, and brand assets are the property of their respective owners.

Video Player

Challenges

The biggest challenge was creating animations that truly captured children's attention. To produce a bumper that feels exciting and impossible to look away from, I conducted thorough research and refined my ideas beforehand.

The character design is intentionally simple—a cube with arms and legs—but I aimed to give it a cute and friendly personality. I also incorporated background elements to clearly express that the character is flying through the sky, adding a playful sense of motion.
